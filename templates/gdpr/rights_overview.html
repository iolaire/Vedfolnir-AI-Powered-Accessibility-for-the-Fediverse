Your Privacy Rights

Understanding your rights under GDPR and other privacy laws
Your Rights Matter
You have fundamental rights regarding your personal data. This page explains each right and how to exercise them with Vedfolnir.
Right to Access
GDPR Article 15

You have the right to know what personal data we have about you and how we use it.

What you can get:
  • All your personal data
  • How we use your data
  • Who we share it with
  • How long we keep it
Export My Data
Right to Rectification
GDPR Article 16

You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

What you can do:
  • Correct wrong information
  • Complete missing data
  • Update outdated information
  • Fix spelling errors
Correct My Data
Right to Erasure
GDPR Article 17

You have the right to have your personal data deleted under certain circumstances.

When you can delete:
  • Data no longer needed
  • You withdraw consent
  • Data processed unlawfully
  • Legal obligation requires it
Delete My Data
Right to Restrict Processing
GDPR Article 18

You have the right to limit how we process your data under certain circumstances.

When you can restrict:
  • You contest data accuracy
  • Processing is unlawful
  • We no longer need the data
  • You object to processing
Request Restriction
Right to Data Portability
GDPR Article 20

You have the right to receive your data in a portable format or transfer it to another service.

What you get:
  • Machine-readable format
  • Structured data export
  • Easy to import elsewhere
  • Your data, your choice
Port My Data
Right to Object
GDPR Article 21

You have the right to object to certain types of data processing.

What you can object to:
  • Direct marketing
  • Profiling for marketing
  • Processing for legitimate interests
  • Research and statistics
Object to Processing
Right to Withdraw Consent
GDPR Article 7(3)

You have the right to withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

Important notes:
  • Easy as giving consent
  • No penalty for withdrawal
  • Doesn't affect past processing
  • May limit service features
Manage Consent
Right to Lodge a Complaint
GDPR Article 77

You have the right to file a complaint with a data protection authority.

When to complain:
  • We don't respond to requests
  • You're not satisfied with our response
  • You believe we violated your rights
  • You have privacy concerns
File Complaint

How to Exercise Your Rights

Step 1: Verify Identity

Log into your account to verify your identity. This protects your privacy and ensures only you can access your data.

Step 2: Choose Your Right

Select the privacy right you want to exercise using the buttons above or through your profile settings.

Step 3: We Respond

We'll process your request within 30 days (or 1 month) as required by GDPR and notify you of the outcome.

Important Information

Response Times
  • Standard: 30 days maximum
  • Complex requests: May extend to 60 days
  • Urgent matters: We prioritize security issues
Costs
  • First request: Always free
  • Repeated requests: Usually free
  • Excessive requests: May charge reasonable fee
Identity Verification

We may ask for additional verification if we have reasonable doubts about your identity, especially for sensitive requests like data deletion.

Need Help?

If you're unsure about any of your rights or need assistance, please contact our privacy team through the privacy request form.
